Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti and pancetta with a silky cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic and then remove garlic when done
  3. Add the spaghetti to the pan with the pancetta
  4. Mix the cheese with the eggs
  5. Take the spaghetti and pancetta pan off the heat, and slowly mix in the egg and cheese mixture
  6. Use tongs to mix the spaghetti around so it is all coated
  7. Add a little bit of the pasta water to make it more saucy
