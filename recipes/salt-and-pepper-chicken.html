Salt and Pepper Chicken

Description

Chicken with Salt and Pepper

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Peppercorns
  • Asian Greens

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into strips and mix with salt and peppercorns and flour
  2. Cook the chicken until golden brown
  3. Mix soy sauce and honey and add to the pan, along with the Asian greens
  4. Cook for a few more minutes until everything is coated and greens are cooked
  5. Serve with rice and garnish with some spring onion
