Egg Fried Rice

Description

Rice cooked with egg and spring onion to garnish

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Spring Onion

Steps

  1. Use leftover rice or rice cooked earlier and left to dry
  2. Heat oil in a wok and then add the onions and fry until lightly browned
  3. Add the rice to the wok and fry for a few minutes
  4. Make a well in the middle and add the egg mixture
  5. Leave the egg to cook for a little bit then mix in with the rice
  6. Serve in a bowl
  7. Add the spring onion at the end
